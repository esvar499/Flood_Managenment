About Us

Flooding is one of the most devastating natural disasters, often leaving affected communities in dire need of support. The Flood Disaster Helpline System provides a platform for affected individuals to quickly access critical information and request help. Our mission is to streamline communication between victims and disaster management agencies, improving response times and saving lives.

  • Quick Response: Connects victims with emergency teams in real-time.
  • Location-Based Help: Request aid based on your area.
  • 24/7 Support: Immediate assistance for those in crisis.
  • Community Support: Encourages volunteers to participate.

Our Services

Emergency Assistance
  • Request immediate rescue and relief support.
  • Get connected to emergency responders.
  • Access first-aid and medical help.
Disaster Reporting
  • Report flood incidents with location details.
  • Submit photos/videos for quick assessment.
  • Alert authorities for immediate action.
Relief and Shelters
  • Find nearby safe shelters.
  • Locate food and water distribution points.
  • Get real-time updates on relief camps.
Real-Time Alerts
  • Receive weather and flood warnings.

  • Stay informed about rescue operations.
Volunteer Coordination
  • Connect volunteers with relief teams.
  • Organize donation drives and fundraisers.
  • Facilitate community support initiatives.
Communication Support
  • Enable direct contact with emergency services.
